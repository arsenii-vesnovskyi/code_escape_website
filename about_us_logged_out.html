About Us

CodeEscape is on a mission to revolutionize the way people learn coding. Our goal is to make learning web development interactive and engaging through escape room challenges that teach HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in a fun and immersive way.

Founded in June 2024 by a group of students from ESADE Business School, our first major milestone was learning how PHP works.

At CodeEscape, we offer interactive learning experiences, progress tracking, and custom avatars to keep our users motivated and engaged.

Located in the vibrant city of Barcelona, we are committed to providing an innovative and modern learning environment. Our core values include creativity, engagement, and continuous improvement, all aimed at making the learning process enjoyable and effective.

Our future plans are bold and ambitious. We aim to revolutionize the way people learn coding and bring new and exciting features to our platform.

Contact us at info@codeescape.com for more information or any inquiries. Alternatively, you can reach us through our Contact Us page.
